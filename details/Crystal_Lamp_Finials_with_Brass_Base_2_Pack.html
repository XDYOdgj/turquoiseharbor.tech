Elegant Crystal Lamp Finials with Polished Brass Base (2 Pack)

Each finial stands at 2 inches tall and is designed with a 1/4-27 thread, ensuring compatibility with standard lamp harps.

Ideal for replacing damaged or missing finials, these decorative accessories can effortlessly upgrade the look of your table lamp, floor lamp, or bookshelf lamp.
