GOEUOU - Replacement Blade and 16 oz Cup Set for Ninja Blender

Perfectly designed for compatibility with a variety of Nutri Ninja blenders, this set includes 2 durable blades and 2 16 oz cups, ideal for making smoothies and shakes with ease.

What It Works With

This replacement set is specifically tailored for Nutri Ninja blenders such as BL770, BL771, BL772, BL740, BL780, BL660, and BL663. It fits the 3.35 inch base size and is not produced or sold by Nutri Ninja.

Built to Last

Made from high-quality food-grade PP and stainless steel, these blades ensure long-lasting performance. They efficiently blend fruits, vegetables, superfoods, ice, and protein shakes into a perfectly smooth consistency. Each blade has been rigorously tested for safety and reliability.

Simple & Hygienic

Designed for effortless use, these cups and blades attach seamlessly to your blender. They are BPA-free and dishwasher safe, making cleaning a breeze. Ideal for a quick and healthy lifestyle.

Important Details

Ensure that your blender has a 6-fins blade base before purchasing. This set is not compatible with 7-fins blades. It fits the 3.35 inch diameter and has 4 blades. Not suitable for Ninja blenders with 18 oz, 24 oz, or 32 oz cups.

What's in the Box

2 x Single Serve Blade | 2 x 16 oz Cup with Lid
