8 Pack Torch Canisters - 16oz Replacement Fuel Canisters with Wicks & Caps

Perfect for outdoor events, garden decor, and DIY lighting projects

Premium Quality Torch Canisters

Sturdy and lightweight, our 16oz torch canisters are crafted from high-quality metal for long-lasting performance. The wide mouth design makes refilling easy and convenient, ensuring a seamless experience for your torch maintenance.

Effortless Refueling

Designed for simplicity, these citronella torch fuel canisters require no tools for refilling. Simply unscrew the cap and pour in the fuel, saving time and effort while minimizing fuel waste. Ideal for regular use and budget-friendly solutions.

Long-Lasting Fiberglass Wicks

Each canister comes with superior fiberglass wicks that burn longer than standard cotton wicks. This means fewer replacements and easier maintenance, making them a reliable choice for all your outdoor lighting needs.

Versatile Outdoor Lighting Solutions

Whether you're hosting a luau, backyard gathering, or creating DIY garden decor, these torch canisters offer a stylish and functional lighting option. Their warm glow adds a romantic touch to any outdoor space or event.

Dual-Use Torch Canisters

These canisters are perfect for use as standalone table torches or as replacements for existing torches. They're flexible enough to fit your home, garden, or party decor, making them an essential addition to your outdoor lighting collection.

Pro Tips for Optimal Use

For best results, avoid over-tightening the canister cap to ensure easy resealing. Always allow the canister to cool completely before opening. Soak the new wick in torch fuel for a few minutes before lighting for consistent performance.
