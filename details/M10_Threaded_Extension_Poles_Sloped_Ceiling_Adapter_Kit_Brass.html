MAYOMAJO M10 Threaded Extension Poles & 180 Degrees Sloped Ceiling Adapter Kit (Brass)

Elevate your lighting design with the MAYOMAJO M10 Threaded Extension Poles Kit. Crafted in premium brass, this versatile lighting accessory set delivers both style and functionality for a wide range of lighting fixtures. Whether you're upgrading your pendant lights, chandeliers, or island lighting, these high-quality extension poles are built to last with corrosion-resistant and oxidation-resistant materials.

The set includes two 12-inch extension rods and two 6-inch rods, offering customizable height adjustments for your lighting installations. Designed with M10 threads, this kit ensures a perfect fit for most ceiling light fixtures, providing a secure and stable connection.

A 180-degree sloped ceiling adapter is also included, allowing for easy installation on sloped ceilings. This adaptable lighting solution is ideal for both new and existing fixtures, making it a perfect addition to any lighting project.

The durable brass construction not only adds a timeless aesthetic to your home interior but also ensures robust support for your lighting without deformation or damage. Perfect for farmhouse, rustic, modern, or industrial decor, these extension poles offer a reliable and attractive lighting upgrade for any space.
