SUNMORY 52" Ceiling Fans with Lights

Modern ceiling fan with smart control, quiet operation, and adjustable lighting for both indoor and outdoor use.

This ceiling fan with light is designed for seamless control. Use your phone with the app or a remote control to manage both the fan and light independently. The versatile control options ensure a tailored and user-friendly experience in any room of your home.

Efficient and Quiet DC Motor

Equipped with a powerful and noiseless DC motor, this fan delivers a strong, consistent airflow while operating at a whisper. The motor is more energy-efficient and quieter than traditional AC models, reducing noise to below 30 dB for a peaceful environment.

Customizable and Bright Lighting

The integrated light fixture offers stepless dimming and a range of color temperatures from warm 3000K to bright 6000K. This ensures the perfect ambiance for any occasion. Additionally, the light has a memory function to keep your preferred settings.

With its elegant design and 52-inch blade diameter, this ceiling fan is perfect for living rooms, bedrooms, dining areas, and patios. The reversible blades feature different textures, adding visual appeal and durability. Ideal for both indoor and outdoor environments, but not recommended for direct exposure to rain.

Reliable After-Sales Support

Enjoy lifetime technical support for your SUNMORY ceiling fan with light. Our dedicated customer service team is ready to assist you with installation guidance or any other needs. Count on us for long-term reliability and satisfaction.
