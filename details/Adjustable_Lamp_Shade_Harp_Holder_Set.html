GIGIVOSHA Lamp-Shade Harp Holder Adjustable Support

Perfectly crafted for versatility, this GIGIVOSHA harp holder is designed to adapt to various lampshade heights. It offers adjustable lengths of 7", 8", 9", and 10", making it ideal for lampshades of similar heights. The detachable and adjustable structure ensures a secure fit for most spider lampshade parts, eliminating the need for precise measurements.

Built to last, the harp and bow are constructed from heavy-duty metal and feature a robust, firm installation. This durability ensures your lighting accessories remain secure and stable, even with frequent use.

Versatile compatibility is another standout feature. The GIGIVOSHA harp holder is designed for E26 and E27 light holders, with a collar. It includes a standard 3/8 inch Uno fitter adapter, allowing you to retrofit your old desk lamps. The set also features a detachable E14 plastic converter for easy switching to E14 bulbs, providing a seamless lighting experience.

Installation is straightforward, requiring minimal effort. Whether you're working with a vintage or modern lamp, this harp holder can be adapted effortlessly for a perfect fit and secure mounting.

Before purchasing, please check that your bulb base is either E26 or E27 with a collar, as shown in the images. This harp holder is specifically tailored for spider lampshades and ensures a stable, adjustable connection for all your lighting needs.
