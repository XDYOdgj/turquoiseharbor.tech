Revorit 11-in-1 Blender Accessories Set

Upgrade your kitchen with this versatile blender replacement parts set designed to work seamlessly with your Nutri Ninja blender. Whether you're blending, crushing, or grinding, this complete accessory kit has you covered with high-quality, durable components that ensure easy and efficient use.

Compatible with a Wide Range of Models

This Revorit accessory kit fits several Nutri Ninja blender models, including BL450_30, BN401, BL482_30, BL910, CT641, CT680, NN210, BL487, BL492_30, BL2012, BL488W, BL486CO, BN751, and BN801. Please verify your machine model matches the compatibility list exactly, including all suffixes, to ensure a perfect fit. Note that models with a 70 suffix are not supported.

All-in-One Blade Assembly

The set includes an advanced blade assembly featuring both a cross blade and a flat blade. The cross blade is ideal for blending fruits, vegetables, and crushing ice to make refreshing smoothies, shakes, and soups. The flat blade is perfect for grinding dry ingredients like spices, grains, and nuts into a fine powder for added versatility in your kitchen routine.

Convenient 24oz Single Serve Cups

Our 24oz cups are designed for single-serve convenience, making it easy to prepare and enjoy your favorite blends on the go. Each cup features clear walls for visible measurement markings, up to 710ml, so you can accurately portion your ingredients every time. The flip lid and straw lid make it simple to drink straight from the cup or use a straw, offering flexibility in how you consume your blends.

Durable and Leak-Proof Design

These blender accessories are built to last with robust, non-toxic materials. The cups, lids, and blades are all dishwasher-safe for quick and easy cleaning, saving you time and effort. Additionally, the set includes 9mm food-grade rubber seals to prevent leaks and ensure airtight storage. A set of cleaning tools is also provided for enhanced handwashing convenience.

Exceptional Customer Support

If you're unsure about your blender model or need assistance, contact our team before purchasing to avoid compatibility issues. We offer a one-year manufacturer warranty and support all customer concerns within 24 hours. Our goal is to provide a seamless, satisfying shopping experience with prompt and reliable service.
