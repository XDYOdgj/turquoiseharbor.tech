Homkin Rattan Pendant Light - 15.75" Matte Black Wicker Hanging Lamp

Elevate your space with this beautifully crafted rattan pendant light that blends modern elegance with bohemian charm. Perfect for both indoor and outdoor use, this hanging lamp adds a unique, artistic touch to any room.

Unique Design

This hand-woven rattan pendant light features a sleek matte black finish that complements any decor style. Its large globe shade emits a warm and evenly distributed light, casting beautiful patterns on your walls that enhance the room's ambiance. The bohemian-inspired design adds a touch of sophistication, making it ideal for creating a cozy and stylish atmosphere.

Easy Installation

Installation is straightforward, thanks to the included mounting hardware. The adjustable cord allows you to customize the length to fit your space. As this fixture requires hardwiring, we recommend hiring a professional electrician for a safe and secure setup.

Premium Specifications

The pendant light measures 15.75" in diameter with a height of 8" for the shade. The cord length is 78.74", making it versatile for various ceiling heights. It is compatible with E26 light bulbs up to 60W, including LED, energy-saving, and halogen options. (Bulb not included)

Versatile Application

This rattan pendant lamp is suitable for a wide range of settings, including living rooms, kitchens, dining areas, hallways, cafes, and outdoor spaces like porches and patios. It adds a decorative element while providing functional lighting, making it a perfect choice for both modern and rustic interiors.

Excellent Customer Service

We offer a 30-day return and exchange policy, giving you peace of mind with your purchase. Additionally, this product comes with a 1-year warranty. If you have any questions or issues, our team is here to help you every step of the way.
