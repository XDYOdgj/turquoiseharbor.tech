JERHOUS White - Ivory Bell Lamp Shades Set of 2

Elevate your lighting with these elegant, handcrafted lamp shades. Designed for a refined look, they add soft illumination and a touch of sophistication to any room.

Key Details

These lamp shades are specifically crafted for use with table lamps or floor lamps that have a harp support system. They are installed in a spider mode, offering a secure and easy fit. Before purchasing, please ensure your existing light fixture is compatible with this style. If not, you may need to acquire a harp for proper installation.

Dimensions

Each shade has a top diameter of 6.7 inches, a bottom diameter of 13 inches, and a height of 10 inches. The spider diameter measures 0.4 inches. Due to the custom sizing, it is essential to verify that these dimensions match your light fixture before ordering.

Material & Craftsmanship

Crafted from a premium blend of white and ivory cotton, polyester, and nylon, these shades are soft to the touch and durable for long-term use. The self-trim top and bottom edges give a clean, finished look, while the expert craftsmanship ensures a high-quality, handcrafted feel. Every shade is made by artisans with over a decade of experience, delivering unmatched attention to detail.

Versatile & Stylish Design

Featuring a rich cream white texture, these bell-shaped lamp shades effortlessly complement a wide range of interior styles, including contemporary, mid-century modern, rustic, vintage, and urban decor. Their elegant silhouette is perfect for adding a sophisticated touch to your table lamp or bedside lamp, transforming your space into a cozy and stylish haven.

Reliable After-Sales Support

JERHOUS offers a 1-year warranty on all lamp shades. If you are not satisfied with your purchase for any reason, our dedicated customer service team is here to assist and provide support whenever needed.

Important Notes

Due to the KD (knock-down) structure of the shade, slight wrinkles may occur on the fabric after assembly. This is completely normal and does not affect the functionality or appearance of the lampshade once properly installed.
