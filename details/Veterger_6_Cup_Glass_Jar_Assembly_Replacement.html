Replacement Parts for Oster Blenders

Designed for effortless home use, these replacement parts include a 6-cup glass jar assembly with blade, gasket, and bottom cap. They are compatible with a wide range of Oster blenders, including the Heritage Blend, Classic Series, Reverse Crush, Perform Blend, Precise Blend 6-Cup, 10-Speed Blender, Rapid Blend, Precise Blend 5-Cup, Pure Blend, Exact Blend, Accurate Blend, and Simple Blend models.

Key Features

  • Replaceable 4-point ice crusher blade (Part # 4961) and 6-point ice crushing blade (Part # 4980)
  • Includes 1 bottom cap, 2 blades, 2 gaskets, and 1 6-cup glass jar
  • Perfect for routine maintenance and repair of your blender
  • Ensures smooth blending performance and longer product life

What You Get

  • 1 x 6-cup glass jar with lid
  • 2 x blades (4-point and 6-point ice crushing)
  • 2 x gaskets for a secure seal
  • 1 x bottom cap for stable jar placement

Note: These parts do NOT fit the Pro, Master, or Versa models.

Why Choose This Set?

Upgrade your blender's performance with high-quality replacement parts that are easy to install and built to last. Whether you're blending smoothies, shakes, or frozen fruits, this assembly delivers reliable operation and a seamless experience. Ideal for home cooks and blend enthusiasts looking to maintain their kitchen appliances without compromising on quality or convenience.
