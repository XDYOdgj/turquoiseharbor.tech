Honoson 16 Pcs Multi Sizes Soccer Paper Lanterns

Soccer Ball Decorations That Bring Energy to Any Celebration

What's Included?

We've packed 16 soccer paper lanterns in four different sizes: 10 inch, 8 inch, 6 inch, and 4 inch. With 4 pieces in each size, these decorations are perfect for any event or display. Whether you're decorating a classroom, a birthday party, or a sports-themed gathering, you'll have just the right number of lanterns to make it shine.

High-Quality and Reusable

These soccer ball party decorations are made from durable tissue paper with metal or plastic wireframes. They are not only lightweight but also easy to store and reuse for multiple occasions. Keep them on hand for birthdays, baby showers, game nights, or any other celebration that needs a lively touch.

Versatile Uses

Whether you're planning a sports-themed party, a birthday bash, a Copa América celebration, or a fun outdoor event, these soccer paper lanterns are the perfect addition. They can also be used to decorate classrooms, bars, game rooms, yards, stadiums, gyms, and children's rooms, making them ideal for a wide range of settings.

How to Use

Setting up is a breeze! Simply expand the lantern carefully and insert the wireframe into the opening. Then, gently thread the wireframe's loop through the eyelets. Be sure to handle them with care to prevent any damage to the lanterns, and you're all set to create a vibrant atmosphere.

Perfect Gift Choice

These colorful soccer paper lanterns are not just decorations—they're also a great gift idea! Send them to friends, family, or loved ones for a unique and thoughtful present. Add a touch of fun and excitement to their day with these playful party accessories.
