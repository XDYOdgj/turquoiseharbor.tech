Zlepmlla Glass Pendant Light

Elevate your space with this stunning industrial-style pendant light. Combining a sleek matte black finish with a beautiful clear seeded glass shade, this fixture effortlessly brings modern aesthetics to your home.

Industrial Mini Design

Featuring a compact and minimalist design, this glass pendant light is a perfect addition to your modern or industrial-inspired interior. The clear bubble glass shade adds a soft, diffused glow while the matte black finish offers a contemporary look. Please confirm the dimensions before ordering to ensure a perfect fit for your space.

Adjustable Height Hanging Light

With its adjustable rope system that measures up to 78.5 inches, this pendant light allows for easy customization to suit your room's layout. Simply pull the cord to your desired length and secure it with the included set screw. It's designed to work seamlessly with both flat and slanted ceilings, making it ideal for hallways, dining rooms, and kitchen islands.

Versatile Bulb Compatibility

This semi-flush pendant light is equipped with an E26 socket, which makes it compatible with a wide range of bulb types such as incandescent, LED, CFL, and halogen. You can choose your favorite bulb to match your lighting needs, and the maximum wattage supported is 60W. Note: Bulbs are not included with the purchase.

Easy Installation

Measuring 11" x 11" x 78.5", this pendant light comes with all necessary mounting hardware and detailed installation instructions, ensuring a quick and hassle-free setup. The canopy diameter of 4.7" further simplifies the installation process.
