White Linen Lampshades Set of 2 - Large Drum Style for Table & Floor Lamps

Upgrade your lighting with our elegant White Linen Lampshades Set of 2. Perfect for adding a touch of sophistication to both table lamps and floor lamps, these drum-shaped shades feature a classic design that complements a wide range of interior styles and decors. Crafted from high-quality linen, they diffuse light beautifully, creating a warm and inviting ambiance in any room.

The 16" top diameter, 16" bottom diameter, and 12" height make these lampshades ideal for most fixtures. However, we recommend double-checking the dimensions to ensure they fit your specific light setup. These shades come in a convenient set, making them perfect for updating multiple light sources at once.

Designed for easy installation, these lampshades are compatible with Spider and Harp systems. They are simple to assemble and can be quickly attached to metal rings using clips, offering a hassle-free way to enhance your lighting. For optimal use, ensure your fixture supports Spider mode.

With their soft linen finish and contemporary shape, these lampshades are not only functional but also add a refined aesthetic to your home. They are built to last with strict quality checks to ensure no surface stains or defects. The durable packaging ensures safe delivery, keeping the shades in perfect condition.
