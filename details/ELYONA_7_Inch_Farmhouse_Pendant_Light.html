ELYONA Woven Pendant Light Fixtures

Elevate your home with the ELYONA 7" Farmhouse Coastal Pendant Light. This modern boho design blends minimalist geometry with natural handwoven rattan, creating a beautiful focal point for any room.

Modern Boho Design

The ELYONA Pendant Light features a sleek, matte black basket shade that enhances the natural textures of handwoven rattan. It effortlessly complements a variety of styles, including modern, traditional, and bohemian, bringing a cozy, inviting ambiance to your kitchen, dining area, or living space. This fixture is the perfect way to add a touch of nature and serenity to your home.

Adjustable Height & Easy Installation

With a maximum height of 56" and a width of 7", this pendant can be customized to fit your space. The included cords allow for height adjustment, ensuring a perfect fit for any ceiling or bevel. Simple installation makes it ideal for both DIY enthusiasts and professional installers alike.

Safe & Dimmable Lighting

Every ELYONA lighting fixture is ETL Listed and meets 120-volt UL safety standards. It is designed for high-quality performance and long-lasting use. This pendant light is dimmable and can be paired with LED, CFL, or Edison bulbs (ETL Listed, E26 Base, Max 40W) for adjustable lighting levels. (Bulbs NOT Included)

Crafted with Care

Our team meticulously selects only the finest materials to ensure durability and aesthetic appeal. The natural twine shade adds a warm, organic feel to any interior, while the clean, contemporary design keeps your space looking fresh and stylish. ELYONA is committed to quality, sustainability, and creating a peaceful living environment.
