JOYSTAR Replacement Blade with 6 Fins Plastic Gear on Bottom (FEMALE FINS ONLY)

Upgrade your Nutri Ninja blender with our high-quality replacement blade designed for seamless performance and durability.

This blade is specifically crafted for the Nutri Ninja Pro and Auto-iQ Blender models. It features 6 fins with a plastic gear on the bottom, ensuring smooth blending and efficient operation. Perfect for users who rely on their blenders for daily smoothies, shakes, and more.

Compatible Models:

  • Nutri Ninja Pro BL451 30 DOB
  • Nutri Ninja Pro BL455 30 DOB
  • Nutri Ninja Pro BL456 BRN 30 DOB
  • Nutri Ninja Auto-iQ BL482 30 DOB
  • Nutri Ninja Auto-iQ BL480D (1)

Key Features:

  • Constructed with durable materials for long-lasting use
  • Designed to fit precisely into your blender assembly
  • Easy to install and replace without professional help
  • Perfect for everyday kitchen use and blending needs
  • Ensure optimal performance with the correct blade configuration

FEMALE FINS ONLY:

Before purchasing, please double-check your existing blade. Count the fins to confirm they are "FEMALE" type. This blade is not for all models; it is specifically designed for those requiring FEMALE fins with a 4" (10cm) diameter. Review all images on the product detail page to ensure compatibility with your blender.
