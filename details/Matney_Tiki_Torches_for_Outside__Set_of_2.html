Matney Stainless Steel Tiki Torches for Outdoor Use

Elevate your outdoor gatherings with these elegant and functional tiki torches. Designed for both style and safety, they bring a warm glow to your patio, garden, or backyard while keeping insects at bay.

Key Features

  • Stainless steel construction ensures durability and resistance to weather elements.
  • 3-piece design includes a fiberglass wick for even burn and a snuffer cap for easy extinguishing.
  • Speared bottom stakes firmly into the ground, providing stability for long-lasting use.
  • Adjustable height allows customization to suit your outdoor space.
  • Measures about 5 feet in height, with a 1.6 cm thick pole for added strength.

Why Choose Matney Tiki Torches?

  • Perfect for creating a cozy and inviting outdoor atmosphere during summer evenings.
  • Use citronella oil to effectively repel mosquitoes and other insects, keeping your space safe and enjoyable.
  • Full flame lasts over 5 hours, even in light wind, for long-lasting illumination.
  • The convenient pour system allows easy refilling with fewer spills – simply use a funnel or cone-shaped container.
  • Fill up to 380 ml maximum, and always ensure you use citronella oil only – never combustible or hazardous oils.

Perfect for Any Outdoor Celebration

These tiki torches are ideal for backyard decorating, patio lighting, and garden ambiance. Whether you're hosting a Hawaiian luau, a tropical-themed party, or simply enjoying a quiet evening with friends, they add a decorative touch that complements any setting.

Great for events like picnics, camping, BBQs, concerts, weddings, and holidays such as July 4th, Memorial Day, and Labor Day. The elegant cone shape makes them a standout feature in any outdoor decor setup.

For outdoor use only. Oil is not included, so be sure to stock up on citronella oil to enjoy the full benefits of these torches.
