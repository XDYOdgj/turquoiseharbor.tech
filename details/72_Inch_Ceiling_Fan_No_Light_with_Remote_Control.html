RIENAZA 72 Inch Ceiling Fan No Light - 8 Blades, DC Reversible Motor, 6 Speeds & Remote Control

Bring cool, quiet, and stylish airflow to your large home or commercial space with the RIENAZA 72 inch ceiling fan. Designed for both indoor and outdoor use, this powerful fan is perfect for living rooms, patios, garages, and more.

Effortless Cooling for Big Spaces

The 72 inch ceiling fan from RIENAZA is built to deliver powerful, consistent airflow across large areas. With 8 rugged aluminum blades, it ensures stable performance and efficient cooling, making it an excellent choice for open spaces and high-ceiling rooms.

Smart Controls for Ultimate Comfort

Enjoy the convenience of a 6-speed remote control and a built-in timer, allowing you to customize airflow intensity and usage time effortlessly. The DC reversible motor provides two airflow directions—ideal for cooling in summer and air circulation in winter.

3 Downrod Options for Versatile Installation

RIENAZA offers 3 downrod lengths—6 inches, 12 inches, and 24 inches—to perfectly suit your ceiling height. Choose the right option for a secure and stylish installation in any room, whether it’s a living room, garage, or outdoor space.

Built for Indoor and Outdoor Use

This oversized ceiling fan is designed to handle both indoor and outdoor environments, making it suitable for patios, breezeways, gazebos, and more. It’s not intended for use in direct rain, but it can withstand the elements in covered outdoor areas.

Easy to Install, No Compromise on Quality

Install your RIENAZA ceiling fan quickly and easily. Our fan comes with detailed installation instructions and a comprehensive user manual. It’s compatible with flat and sloped ceilings, with a maximum slope of 15 degrees.

Unmatched After-Sales Support

We stand behind our products with a lifetime warranty on the motor and rigorous quality checks to ensure a smooth, balanced, and quiet operation. With support centers in North America, Europe, and East Asia, RIENAZA guarantees a seamless experience from purchase to use.

Perfect for Every Room
