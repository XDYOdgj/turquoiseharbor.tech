Leadigol Paper Star Lantern Decoration

Elevate your space with our 6-piece set of 3D cut-out paper star lanterns. Each lantern is designed with elegant star and moon patterns, creating a magical ambiance that transforms any room into a celebration of light and style. Whether you're decorating for a wedding, Christmas, or a birthday party, these lanterns are perfect for adding a touch of whimsy and festivity.

Crafted from durable, prismatic optical reflective card paper, these lanterns are not only eye-catching but also reusable. The unique design allows for easy assembly and disassembly, making them a versatile decoration for any occasion. Simply hang them with the included rope and let the light shine through for a stunning nighttime effect.

These lanterns can be illuminated using tea lanterns or string lights (not included), making them ideal for creating a warm, inviting glow. Perfect for indoor use, they can be hung on walls, trees, or ceilings, bringing the charm of star lighting into your home or outdoor space.

Each lantern measures 11.8 inches (30 cm) when unfolded and 5.9 inches (15 cm) when folded, making them easy to store and transport. The set includes 6 different colored lanterns: golden, green, slivery, rose, red, and blue, ensuring a variety of styles to suit your preferences.

Whether you're looking to add a festive touch or create a romantic setting, these paper star lanterns are a delightful addition to your decor. They are the perfect blend of creativity, elegance, and practicality for any celebration.
