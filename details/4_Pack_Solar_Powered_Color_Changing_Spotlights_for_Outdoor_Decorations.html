GVSHINE 4 Pack Color Changing Solar Spotlights

Elevate your outdoor décor with these smart, solar-powered spotlights designed for beauty and functionality. Perfect for trees, houses, statues, and more.

Focused Beam & Sunset Glow

These high-quality solar landscape spotlights are engineered with an oversized round lens that provides focused, even lighting with clear boundaries. Experience a warm, sunset-like illumination that reduces glare and enhances your nighttime comfort. Simply charge them under direct sunlight for at least 12 hours before first use.

8 Color Modes for Every Holiday

With eight dynamic color modes—auto-cycle, white, red, green, blue, yellow, purple, and cyan—these lights are the perfect addition to your festive or seasonal décor. Use them to create a vibrant atmosphere for holidays like Halloween, Christmas, or any special occasion, adding a magical glow to your garden, path, or driveway.

Bright & Wide Lighting Coverage

These solar floodlights offer a powerful beam that can illuminate up to 35 feet, making them ideal for large outdoor spaces. When mounted properly, they can project a wide range of light patterns, from 2 to 20 feet in diameter, effectively lighting up statues, trees, and creating an eye-catching contrast with sharp shadows.

Durable & Weather-Resistant Design

Equipped with an IP65 waterproof rating, these solar spotlights are built to last through all seasons. GVSHINE’s latest model includes a large 6.3*4.2 inch solar panel and a 2200mAh high-capacity battery, ensuring longer runtime and reliable performance even in overcast conditions.

Adjustable Solar Panel & Light Angle

These lanterns feature an independently adjustable solar panel and light head. Rotate the solar panel up or down by 135° for optimal sunlight capture during the day, and adjust the light by 90° to illuminate any desired area at night—perfect for personalizing your lighting setup.

Simple & Hassle-Free Installation

Install these solar spotlights in just a few seconds. You can easily place them in the ground or use the included screws to secure them to a wall—no wiring or professional installation required. Ideal for use in areas that get regular mowing or maintenance.

All Day Power, Zero Extra Costs

Powered entirely by the sun, these lights eliminate the need for electricity, saving you money and hassle. No bulb replacements, minimal maintenance, and a worry-free experience that brings sustainable lighting to your outdoor spaces.

3 Important Tips for Best Performance

  • Turn the spotlight on and charge it for a full day before using it for the first time.
  • Keep the light away from artificial sources
