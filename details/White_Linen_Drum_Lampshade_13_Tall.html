YoflowX White Linen Tall Drum Lampshade

Elevate your lighting style with our handcrafted, elegant lampshade. Designed for both table and floor lamps, along with pendant lights, this 10" Top x 10" Bottom x 13" height drum lampshade offers a timeless look that blends seamlessly with any interior decor.

Perfect Fit for Your Lighting Fixtures

This large-size lampshade is compatible with most table lamps, floor lamps, and wall lamps that use lamp harps. Its screw thread diameter of 1.65" ensures secure attachment. We recommend checking your lamp holder size before purchasing to ensure compatibility.

Easy to Install and Remove

Featuring a detachable design, this lampshade is perfect for hassle-free product transportation and storage. You can quickly secure it around the metal rings using the clips, and set it up in just one minute without the need for any tools.

Luxurious and Durable Material

Handcrafted from premium linen, this tall drum lampshade is both soft to the touch and long-lasting. The surface is specially treated to resist stains and daily wear, making it simple to maintain. A sturdy metal frame adds extra durability, ensuring this shade can last for decades.

Versatile and Stylish Design

The natural white color offers a clean and elegant aesthetic that complements modern, rustic, vintage, and urban home styles. Whether placed on a nightstand, beside a bed, or in a living room, this lampshade adds a soothing glow and a touch of refined beauty to any space.

Customizable Options to Suit Your Taste

Choose from three classic colors: black, white, and beige. For a more personalized touch, we offer customized lampshade solutions to reflect your unique style and enhance your home decor.
