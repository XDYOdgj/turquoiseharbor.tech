HILUXIA 18" Modern Double Drum Fabric Pendant Light

Elevate your space with this modern chandelier that blends simplicity and elegance. The double-tier drum design features a soft and warm beige linen fabric shade, casting a cozy glow that enhances any room's ambiance. This fixture is a perfect fit for dining rooms, kitchens, living rooms, or bedrooms, adding a touch of sophistication to your interior decor.
