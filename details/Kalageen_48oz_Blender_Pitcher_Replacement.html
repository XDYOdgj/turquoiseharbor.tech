Kalageen 48oz Blender Pitcher - Classic Explorian Series

Discover the perfect replacement for your blender with the Kalageen 48oz pitcher. Not compatible with commercial series or Vita Prep blends.

Space-Saving Design for Easy Storage

Measuring just 10.5 inches in height, this pitcher is designed to fit effortlessly under overhead cabinets. When attached to your blender, the total height is about 18.5 inches, which is ideal for small kitchens. Keep your workspace clean and organized by storing this pitcher away when not in use, reducing clutter and dust buildup.

Ideal for Small to Medium Families

Perfect for creating delicious smoothies, soups, frozen desserts, and more, this 48oz (6 cup) pitcher is ideal for small to medium households. It allows you to make thoughtful, personalized meals without waste, enhancing your daily routine with convenience and style.

Dual-Use Containers for More Than Just Smoothies

Pair this 48oz pitcher with the 64oz container for different uses on the same blender. The distinct designs help you easily differentiate between them, ensuring accurate measurements and preventing cross-contamination. Ideal for making baby food or preparing allergen-sensitive meals with confidence.

Versatile Tool for Every Kitchen Need

Designed for daily use, the Kalageen 48oz pitcher is a versatile tool for blending everything from food to non-food items like pet treats or body scrubs. Its high-quality construction ensures long-lasting performance, while its affordable price makes it a smart choice for any kitchen.
