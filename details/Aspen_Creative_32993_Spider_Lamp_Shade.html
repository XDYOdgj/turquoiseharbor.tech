Aspen Creative 32993 Handwoven Empire Spider Lamp Shade

Add a timeless touch of elegance to your lighting with this beautifully crafted handwoven Empire spider lamp shade from Aspen Creative. Made from natural rattan, this unique shade features a classic weave pattern that brings warmth and texture to any room.

With a 13" top diameter, 16" bottom diameter, and 11" slant height, the shade is designed to complement a wide range of lamp bases. It comes with a brown brass spider fitter, making it easy to install on a compatible lamp harp and finial (sold separately).

Each lamp shade is meticulously handmade, ensuring that every piece is one-of-a-kind. Slight variations in size and color are part of the charm and authenticity of the natural rattan material.

Aspen Creative offers a diverse range of lighting solutions that blend style with affordability. From portable lamps to pendant lights, our collection includes everything you need to make your space shine. Our design team keeps up with the latest trends to create products that are both functional and beautiful.
