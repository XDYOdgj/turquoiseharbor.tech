WBinDX Woven Rattan Pendant Light Fixture

A modern twist on rustic charm with two stunning boho pendant lights that bring warmth, texture, and style to your space.

Boho Rattan Pendant Lights

These 2-pack pendant lights are designed with a handwoven basket shade made from natural rattan, creating a soft, ambient glow that filters beautifully through the material. The warm light casts gentle shadows on the ceiling, adding a unique bohemian vibe to your kitchen island, dining area, or bedroom.

Natural and Durable Materials

Featuring a natural wood canopy and a carefully crafted rattan lampshade, these fixtures are built to last. Their anti-rust and fade-resistant properties ensure they maintain their aesthetic appeal over time, blending traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design.

Easy to Install

With no assembly required, these lights are ready to use straight out of the box. Simply install your bulbs and mount the fixture on your ceiling – whether it's flat, sloped, slanted, or vaulted – making them a versatile choice for any room.

Adjustable Height

Each pendant light comes with an adjustable 59'' cord, allowing you to customize the hanging height to fit your space perfectly. The compact size – 7.08'' wide and 7.87'' tall – makes them ideal for small spaces like a kitchen island, hallway, foyer, or entryway.

Versatile Lighting Solution

Designed for both functionality and aesthetics, these lights require just one E26 bulb (max 60W) and are compatible with LED, Edison, CFL, incandescent, or halogen bulbs. They add a cozy atmosphere to any room, and are fully dimmable when paired with a dimmer switch.

Perfect for Multiple Spaces

Whether you're decorating your bedroom, living room, kitchen island, or adding timeless style to your bar or hallway, these farmhouse-style rattan pendant lights are a versatile and eye-catching choice for any interior design project.
