GahsElec Upgraded 24oz Blender Cups for Ninja Blenders

Designed for seamless compatibility with a wide range of Ninja Auto IQ and other series blenders, these upgraded cups are built to last and enhance your blending experience.

Perfect for smoothies, shakes, and more, these cups are ideal for anyone with a busy lifestyle.

Key Features

  • Designed to fit various Ninja blender models, including BL682-30, BL642-30, BL450-30, BL455-30, BL456-30, BL482-30, BL687CO-30 and more.
  • Upgraded with a secure sip & seal lid, preventing leaks during use and making it perfect for on-the-go convenience.
  • Contains 7 fins for efficient blending and is compatible only with 18oz, 24oz, and 32oz cups.
  • Crafted from BPA-free, food-grade PP plastic for safety and durability.
  • Dishwasher safe and easy to clean, saving you time and effort.
  • Includes a rubber gasket to ensure a tight seal and prevent spills.

Why Choose GahsElec?

When you choose GahsElec, you're investing in quality, safety, and convenience. Our upgraded 24oz cups are engineered to enhance your daily blending routine, whether you're making a quick smoothie in the morning or a nutritious shake on the go.

What's Included?

Each package contains 2 x 24oz cups, 2 x upgraded sip & seal lids, 1 x 7-fins blender blade, and 1 x rubber gasket. Everything is designed to work together and deliver a flawless blending experience.

Safe for Your Kitchen & Family

Our cups are made from eco-friendly materials that are safe for use with your food and better for the environment. They are ideal for busy parents, health-conscious individuals, and everyday users who value both quality and sustainability.
