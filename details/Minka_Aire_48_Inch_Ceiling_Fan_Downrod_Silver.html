Minka-Aire 48 Inch Ceiling Fan Downrod - Silver - DR548-SL

Enhance the performance and installation experience of your Minka-Aire ceiling fan with the 48-inch downrod. Specifically engineered for compatibility with Minka-Aire fans, this accessory ensures seamless integration and optimal functionality without the hassle of fitting with other brands.

Featuring a premium silver finish and durable steel construction, this downrod is built to last while adding a touch of elegance to your ceiling fan setup. The 3/4" diameter provides a secure and robust connection, ensuring stability and reliability in any environment.

Designed with convenience in mind, this downrod includes a predrilled port for the lock pin and is threaded on one end for effortless attachment to your ceiling fan coupling. Whether you have a low, medium, or high ceiling, this downrod allows for easy installation and optimal airflow performance from your fan.

Available in a range of sizes and finishes, these downrods are ideal for both indoor and outdoor use. Choose the perfect match to suit your installation needs and elevate the efficiency of your Minka-Aire ceiling fan in any home or space.
