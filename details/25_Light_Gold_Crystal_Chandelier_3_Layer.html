SHAOCHIAO 25-Light Large Gold Crystal Chandelier

Elevate your home's ambiance with this stunning 25-light, large gold crystal chandelier. Featuring a timeless empire style design, it combines elegance and sophistication to create a luxurious focal point in any room.

Handcrafted with premium K9 crystal and a polished metallic frame, this chandelier adds a touch of opulence and modern luxury to your living space. Whether you're looking to accentuate a grand entrance, a high ceiling, or a sophisticated hallway, this fixture is designed to make a statement.

Product Specifications

  • Dimensions: Height 60" x Width 31.1"
  • Adjustable: You can customize the chain length up to 78.7" to fit your specific ceiling height.
  • Weight: Approx 86 pounds. Ensure your ceiling can support this weight before installation.

Lighting Options

This chandelier requires 25 x E12 bulbs (not included), with a maximum wattage of 60W. It is compatible with a wide range of bulb types including dimmable, energy-saving, LED, and halogen bulbs, offering versatility in lighting style and energy efficiency.

Ideal For

Showcasing its impressive size, this lighting fixture is perfect for entryways, foyers, staircases, hallways, living rooms, and even banquet halls. It adds a refined touch to both residential and commercial spaces, enhancing the overall aesthetic and lighting experience.

Installation & Warranty

Each chandelier comes with detailed installation instructions to guide you through the setup. Full assembly is required, but the process is rewarding and ensures a secure, long-lasting installation.
