Product Specifications
  • Color: Chrome
  • Pole Length: 12 Inches
  • Maximum Total Length: 48 Inches
  • Pole Outer Diameter: 11.5mm
  • Inner Diameter: 9.5mm
  • Thread Pitch: 1.0mm

This set is ideal for DIY enthusiasts and interior designers looking to achieve the perfect lighting setup. Whether you're replacing existing parts or adding a new design, these extension poles provide a flexible and easy-to-install solution.

Durable & Reliable

Constructed from thick, corrosion-resistant iron, these extension rods are built to last. Perfect for Any Lighting Setup

These extension poles can be used to adjust the height of your pendant lights, chandeliers, or island lights. The adjustable design allows you to customize the length to suit your specific space and aesthetic. Ideal for both sloped and flat ceilings, this set offers a practical and stylish lighting upgrade solution.

Stylish Addition to Your Space
