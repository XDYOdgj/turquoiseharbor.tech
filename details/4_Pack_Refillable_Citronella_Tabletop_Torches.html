FAN-Torches 4 Pack Glass Tabletop Torches

Elevate your outdoor ambiance with our stylish and functional 4-pack glass tabletop torches. Designed for both beauty and convenience, these flame light torches are perfect for enhancing your yard, patio, garden, or any outdoor event with a warm glow.

High-Quality Glass Construction

Crafted from premium, durable glass, these tabletop torches are not only lightweight but also robust enough to handle outdoor use. The 12 oz fuel capacity ensures a long-lasting burn of over 8 hours, making them ideal for evening gatherings or extended outdoor enjoyment. (Oil Not Included)

Simple and Efficient Refilling

With their wide mouth design, these outdoor torches are easy to fill without any tools. Simply unscrew the canister, and you can easily refuel your patio torches. Each set includes a funnel for hassle-free refills and to prevent fuel spillage, helping you save time and money.

Long-Lasting Fiberglass Wicks

Replaceable fiberglass wicks are included in each package, offering unmatched durability compared to traditional cotton wicks. These wicks last indefinitely with regular use, reducing the need for frequent replacements and ensuring consistent performance for your outdoor lighting needs.

Versatile Decor for Every Occasion

Whether you're hosting a romantic garden party, adding a rustic touch to your backyard, or creating a cozy ambiance for your patio, these citronella torches deliver both practicality and visual appeal. They're perfect for DIY projects, seasonal decor, and special events alike.

Dual-Use Design for Flexibility

These outdoor torches can be used as standalone tabletop lighting or as replacements for other torches. Their versatile design makes them a great addition to your home decor, garden, yard, or any outdoor setting where a warm, inviting light is needed.

Tips for Best Performance

For optimal use, soak the new wick in torch fuel for a few minutes before the first lighting. This ensures a steady flame and maximizes the burn time of each torch.
