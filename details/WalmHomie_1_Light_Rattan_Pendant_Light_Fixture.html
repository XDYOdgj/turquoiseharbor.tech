  • Handwoven rattan design with a touch of white plastic for added flexibility
  • Ideal for modern and farmhouse decor
  • Eco-friendly and long-lasting construction

Perfect for

  • Kitchens, living rooms, dining areas, and bedrooms

  • Hallways, stairwells, libraries, and home offices

This pendant light is not only a functional lighting solution but also a stylish statement that enhances the aesthetic of your home or business. It's designed for easy setup and long-term use, making it an excellent choice for those who value both beauty and practicality.
