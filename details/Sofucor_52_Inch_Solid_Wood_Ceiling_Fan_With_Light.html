Sofucor 52 Inch Solid Wood Ceiling Fan with Light

Combine style with function with this premium ceiling fan that brings comfort and illumination to your home or outdoor space.

Premium Solid Wood Design

Make a statement with the Sofucor 52 Inch Solid Wood Ceiling Fan. Crafted from high-quality natural wood, this modern fan adds elegance and warmth to any room. The 3-blade walnut design is not only eye-catching but also enhances airflow, keeping you cool and comfortable all day long.

Its A+++ energy efficiency rating ensures that it uses less power than traditional fans, helping you save on electricity bills and reduce energy waste by up to 75%.

Experience Year-Round Comfort

Equipped with a whisper-quiet, high-performance DC motor, this fan delivers smooth and powerful airflow. With 6 precise speed settings, you can adjust the breeze to your preference, from a gentle whisper to a strong cooling blast.

The reversible motor feature allows you to change the airflow direction, pushing warm air down in winter and cool air up in summer. This creates a more balanced and comfortable temperature in your space all year round.

Smart Remote Control

Control your fan with ease using the included remote. Adjust speed settings, activate the timer for 1, 2, or 4 hours, and toggle between forward and reverse rotation from the comfort of your seat. You can also turn the fan and light on separately, offering greater flexibility in use.

Once installed, the remote can be used as a wall switch, merging convenience with modern aesthetics.

Indoor & Outdoor Versatility

Sofucor’s 52 Inch ceiling fan is designed for both indoor and outdoor use. With 3 adjustable downrods (5", 10", and 24"), it can be tailored to fit various ceiling heights or spaces. The 15-degree tilt feature allows for precise angle adjustments to direct airflow where it’s needed the most.

Perfect for farmhouse, living room, bedroom, kitchen, or outdoor areas like patios and breezeways. Ideal for creating a relaxing ambiance or boosting air circulation in open spaces.

Enjoy a peaceful environment with the Sofucor 52 Inch ceiling fan, which runs quietly at just 35dB—softer than the sound of falling leaves or a soft breeze. This makes it ideal for light sleepers and those who value a calm, noise-free living space.
