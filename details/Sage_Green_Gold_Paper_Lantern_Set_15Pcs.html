YOVIWEI Sage Green Gold Paper Lanterns

Transform your event into a magical space with this stunning 15-piece sage green and gold paper lantern set. With five different sizes (10”, 8”, and 6”), you can create beautiful layered backdrops that are perfect for weddings, baby showers, graduations, baptisms, and other special occasions.

Each lantern is crafted with an elegant blend of sage green, gold, and ivory tones, designed to evoke a sense of timeless romance and sophistication. This set includes 3 sage green, 1 metallic gold-striped, 2 ivory, 3 forest green, and 2 gold lanterns, offering a versatile and eye-catching palette for all your events.

Multipurpose & Versatile Design

Whether it's a romantic wedding ceremony, a cozy baby shower, a stylish graduation celebration, or a grand birthday bash, these lanterns seamlessly adapt to any theme. They're ideal for indoor spaces like classrooms and offices, or for outdoor settings such as gardens and courtyards.

Easy Setup & Reusable

Thanks to the high-quality paper and durable metal frames, these lanterns are built to last. They come with simple setup instructions, making it easy for you to hang them with confidence. The collapsible design ensures hassle-free storage and reuse across different events, from New Year's celebrations to holiday-themed gatherings.

Elegant Hanging Options

These lanterns are designed for both vertical and horizontal hanging, giving you endless possibilities to style your space. Hang them along arches, windows, aisles, or courtyards to add a touch of retro elegance to any gold and green-themed party or forest-inspired event.

Create unforgettable memories with YOVIWEI's handcrafted paper lanterns—perfect for any celebration that needs a touch of vintage charm.
