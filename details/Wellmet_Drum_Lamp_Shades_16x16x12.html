Wellmet Drum Lampshades - Easy Assembly Required

Enhance your lighting with the Wellmet Drum Lampshades, designed for both table and floor lamps. Made from natural linen fabric, these lampshades offer a soft, diffused glow that creates a warm and inviting atmosphere in any room. The openwork polka dot pattern adds a stylish, modern touch while maintaining a classic aesthetic.

Our lampshades are built with care and require simple assembly to ensure they arrive undamaged and ready for use. All necessary mounting hardware and clear instructions are included, making the setup process quick and hassle-free. The metal spider fitter is constructed from durable stainless steel, providing strong support and ensuring your lampshade stays securely in place.

These drum shades are available in five sizes, including the popular 16"x16"x12" option. Please verify the dimensions of your lamp before ordering to ensure the perfect fit. Whether you're decorating your living room, bedroom, or study, these lampshades are versatile and ideal for both modern minimalist and rustic decor styles.

Use them in your home office, entryway, kitchen, or farmhouse for a cohesive and elegant lighting setup. With their timeless design and high-quality materials, the Wellmet Drum Lampshades are the perfect choice for anyone looking to upgrade their lighting with a touch of sophistication and style.
