Qiokhgi White Round Paper Lanterns

Elevate your space with these elegant and versatile 15.75" large hanging paper lanterns. Perfectly suited for both traditional and modern decor, these lanterns are designed to bring a warm and inviting glow to any room or celebration.

Soft and Natural Elegance

Handmade with crumpled rice paper, these lanterns offer a unique texture that adds a soft, ambient light to your surroundings. The shade measures 15.75 inches in diameter, with a top opening of 5 inches and a bottom opening of 3.35 inches. Ideal for creating a cozy and welcoming atmosphere in your home or event setting.

Premium Materials for Long-Lasting Use

Constructed from durable metal supports and high-quality rice paper, these paper lanterns are both sturdy and foldable. They are easy to store and can be reused multiple times, making them a great addition to your DIY projects. Simply insert an LED light (not included) for a safe and stylish lighting solution.

Versatile Decor Options

These paper lanterns are designed for maximum versatility. Hang them on walls, ceilings, beams, or even trees to create a unique lighting display. Ideal for festivals, birthdays, weddings, and other special occasions. Each lantern features a slot to easily insert a string of lights, enhancing the ambiance and evening mood.

Creative and Customizable Lighting

Transform your home or event space with these customizable paper lanterns. Easy to color, glue, and decorate with streamers or floral accents. They also work well with stickers and markers for arts and crafts projects. Perfect for classrooms, living rooms, bedrooms, and New Year decorations. Add a touch of culture and charm to your interior design.

Perfect for Any Occasion

Whether you're planning a festive celebration, a unique party, or a romantic evening, these Qiokhgi lanterns are designed to fit all scenarios. They are ideal for both indoor and outdoor use, adding a decorative touch to porches, patios, and any space that needs a little extra flair.

Important Safety Tips

To ensure safe use, avoid placing bulbs directly in contact with the paper. Halogen bulbs are not recommended. Due to manual manufacturing, slight size variations of 10-20mm may occur. Always follow the manufacturer's guidelines for optimal performance.
