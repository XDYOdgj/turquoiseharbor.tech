Funtuan Replacement Parts for NutriBullet - 15-Piece Upgrade Kit

Upgrade your NutriBullet experience with this essential set of replacement parts. Designed for compatibility with NutriBullet Original (600W) and NB Pro (900W) models, this kit ensures smooth performance and lasting durability.

Compatible & Precision-Fit

This complete set is specifically engineered to work with NutriBullet Original and NB Pro models. It does not fit the NutriBullet Lean or RX versions, so you can be confident in its accuracy and functionality.

Safe & Durable Materials

Manufactured from high-quality, food-grade plastic that is BPA-free and free from bubbles or cracks. You can safely enjoy your favorite fruits and vegetables with peace of mind, knowing your kitchen tools are both safe and reliable.

No Leaking Design

Featuring a secure, leak-proof seal and a removable lid ring, this set gives you a hassle-free way to enjoy your blended creations. The included comfortable handle ensures easy pouring and a smooth drinking experience.

All-in-One Kit

Includes 1 extraction blade, 1 tall cup (24 oz), 1 short cup (18 oz), 1 lid ring, 1 lid ring with handle, 2 flat lids, 3 shock pads, and 1 set of cleaner brushes. Ideal for regular use or for a larger family, this kit covers all your blending needs in one convenient package.

Whether you're a busy parent or a health-conscious individual, this upgrade kit provides the perfect solution for effortless and clean blending every time.

Note: This product is intended for use with NutriBullet Original (600W) and NB Pro (900W) models only. Not suitable for use with hot liquids.
