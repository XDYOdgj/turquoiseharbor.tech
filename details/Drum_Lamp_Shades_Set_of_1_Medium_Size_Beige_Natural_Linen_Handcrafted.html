FASTTOTOTA Drum Lamp Shades - Set of 1

Elevate your lighting with the FASTTOTOTA Drum Lamp Shades. Designed for both style and functionality, these shades feature a classic drum shape with a clean, elegant look that complements modern interiors. The dimensions are carefully crafted: top diameter 11.02 inches, bottom diameter 11.02 inches, and a height of 9.4 inches. The screw thread diameter measures 0.43 inches, making them compatible with most mid-sized table and floor lamps.

Each shade is made from natural linen, offering a soft, textured feel that enhances the ambiance of your space. The exterior is lined with high-quality fabric, ensuring a smooth and durable finish. The interior is wrapped in thick polyethylene for added protection and longevity. The solid metal frame adds structural support, guaranteeing stability and durability for years to come.

For easy installation, the lamp shades come rolled up for safe transport. Simply unfold them and secure them on the metal rings by fitting into the clips. Assembly takes just a minute, allowing you to enjoy the benefits of soft lighting without the hassle of complicated setup.

The FASTTOTOTA Lamp Shades are designed with a spider fitter type, resting on top of harps and fixed with screw-on finials. This ensures a secure and stable fit on your light fixtures. Whether you're looking for a subtle accent or a bold statement, these shades provide excellent light diffusion, softening the glow and enhancing the overall atmosphere of your room.

As a brand committed to quality and customer satisfaction, FASTTOTOTA offers a one-year warranty on all its products. We are dedicated to creating personalized, elegant, and creative lighting solutions. If for any reason you're not fully satisfied, our team is here to resolve your issues with prompt and friendly service.
