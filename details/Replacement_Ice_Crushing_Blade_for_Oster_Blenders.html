Replacement Ice Crushing Blade for Oster Blender

Are you looking for a reliable, high-quality replacement blade for your Oster blender? Our 4961-011 Ice Crushing Blade is designed to deliver smooth, powerful blending performance with durability and precision.

Sturdy & Durable Construction

This blade is crafted from premium stainless steel to ensure it's sharp enough for crushing ice and other tough ingredients with ease. The jar base cap is made of hard plastic, which is resistant to cracking and ensures long-lasting use. Additionally, the included 2 pieces of rubber O-Ring Gaskets provide a secure, leak-proof seal for hassle-free operation.

Perfect Fit for Oster & Osterizer Blenders

Our replacement parts kit is compatible with most Oster and Osterizer blenders, including 5 and 6 cup jars made of glass or plastic. The blade's coupling and square drive pin are made from food-grade stainless steel, ensuring safe and smooth function. The 4-point design allows for even distribution of force, making it ideal for pulverizing and chopping ingredients with precision.

Effortless Installation & Long-Lasting Use

The O-Ring gasket is installed at the bottom of the blade plate and is built to last. It's compatible with most Oster kitchen centers, though not with the Oster Pro 1200 model. This blade is built to last, so you can trust it to handle regular use without wear or tear.

Enhance Your Blending Experience

With this ice crushing blade, you can achieve perfectly blended smoothies, shakes, and other frozen ingredients in no time. It's an essential accessory for anyone who wants to maintain their blender's performance and extend its lifespan.
