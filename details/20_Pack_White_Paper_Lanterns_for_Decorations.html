20 Pcs White Paper Lanterns – Stylish Decor with 12 Color Pens

Transform your space with these beautifully crafted white paper lanterns. Each lantern can be customized with the included 12 color pens, making them perfect for creative DIY projects. The set includes 5 different white series, ensuring you can match your decor style with ease.

High-Quality & Durable Materials

These paper lanterns are made from premium quality paper that is sturdy yet elegant. Designed to be hung on walls, trees, ceilings, or gate covers, they are ideal for both indoor and outdoor use. The vibrant colors enhance the ambiance of any gathering, adding a soft and magical light that is sure to make a lasting impression.

Easy to Assemble & Disassemble

Installation is simple: just open the lantern and insert the metal frame to create the desired shape. Disassembling is equally hassle-free, requiring only a gentle press to remove the frame. Creative DIY Gifts

With no pre-printed patterns, these lanterns are a canvas for your creativity. Whether you're writing a message, drawing a design, or adding a personal touch, these white paper lanterns make for meaningful and memorable gifts for family, friends, and loved ones.

Versatile Decor Options

Use these colorful paper lanterns to decorate for a variety of occasions such as birthdays, weddings, Halloween, Chinese New Year, Easter, and more. Ideal for classroom setups, festivals, and parties, they bring a touch of warmth, style, and atmosphere to any event or decorating project.
