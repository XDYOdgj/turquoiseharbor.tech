Product Description

Add a touch of elegance and rustic charm to your lighting setup with the Aspen Creative 51003-2 Empire Clip-On Lamp Shades. These shades are made from premium natural rattan, featuring a unique weave design that adds texture and visual interest to any room. The shape is inspired by the timeless European style, making it a versatile addition to modern or traditional interiors.

Each shade measures 3" at the top, 6" at the bottom, and has a slant height of 5-1/4", ensuring a perfect fit for your chandelier. These shades come in a set of 2 and are designed to work with 40-watt light bulbs (bulbs are not included). They are compatible with E12 base torpedo bulbs, and a clip-on fitter makes installation easy and secure.
