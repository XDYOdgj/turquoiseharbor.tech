TaoTazon Bamboo Citronella Torch Replacement Canisters (16oz)

Create a warm and inviting outdoor atmosphere with these high-quality torch canisters. Perfect for patio parties, garden decorating, or adding a touch of elegance to your outdoor spaces, these canisters are designed to enhance your summer experience with lasting light and a natural, eco-friendly style.

16 Ounce Large Capacity

These upgraded torch canisters feature a large 16oz capacity, delivering extended burn time and fewer refills. Equipped with a convenient snuff cap, they make it easy to extinguish the flame and keep your outdoor lighting safe and ready for use at a moment's notice.

Easy Pour System

The wide mouth design allows for effortless fueling, making it simple to refill your torches quickly. This system also minimizes fuel spillage and waste, ensuring a smoother, more convenient experience for your garden or outdoor gatherings. The clean, polished surface adds a subtle decorative touch that complements any setting.

Included Fiberglass Wicks

These canisters come with durable fiberglass wicks that offer long-lasting performance without the risk of burning out like cotton wicks. Fiberglass helps prevent fuel contamination and ensures a steady, clean flame, reducing maintenance and saving you time and money over time.
