Rosient 16 Inch Large Flush Mount Ceiling Light Fixture with Seeded Glass

Elevate your home decor with the Rosient 16-inch flush mount ceiling light. This beautifully designed fixture features a round, black metal frame paired with a charming seeded glass shade, offering a perfect blend of modern craftsmanship and classic appeal. Ideal for both indoor and outdoor spaces, it brings a touch of farmhouse style wherever it's installed.

Measuring 16.1 inches in diameter and 5.3 inches in height, this light is designed to fit seamlessly into your ceiling without taking up much space. Its compact yet eye-catching silhouette makes it a great choice for kitchens, dining areas, living rooms, foyers, entryways, and more.

The Rosient ceiling light is built with durability and functionality in mind. Installing this light couldn't be easier. Rosient provides all necessary hardware and clear instructions, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free setup, whether you're a seasoned DIYer or just starting out.
