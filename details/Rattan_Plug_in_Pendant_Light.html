YXTH Plug in Pendant Light

Elevate your home decor with the YXTH Plug in Pendant Light, a stunning blend of natural rattan craftsmanship and modern functionality. This hand-woven wicker hanging lamp brings a bohemian touch to your living space, adding both style and warm, ambient lighting with its 15-foot dimmable cord. Perfect for farmhouse bedrooms, it's designed to complement your room's aesthetic while providing versatile lighting options.

Natural & Handmade Design

Featuring a pure handmade rattan lampshade, this plug in pendant light emits a smooth and well-proportioned glow. The textured braided thread adds depth and character to the design, making it a unique and eye-catching piece for any room. Its boho-inspired look effortlessly blends with rustic, modern, and eclectic interior styles.

Adjustable Brightness for Any Mood

With a dimmable feature and a 1%-100% brightness range, this plug in hanging light fixture allows you to customize the lighting to suit your needs. Whether you want a soft ambiance for relaxation or a brighter setting for tasks, the adjustable switch lets you control the atmosphere without difficulty.

Versatile Use Across Rooms

Ideal for a wide range of applications, this boho hanging lamp delivers natural, soft, and warm light when turned on. It adds a beautiful accent to dining rooms, living rooms, bedrooms, kitchens, and even island settings. Its minimalist yet artistic design makes it a perfect choice for any room that needs a touch of elegance and charm.

Simple & Quick Installation

Installing the YXTH pendant light is easy and hassle-free. Simply tighten the lamp holder and shade, then hang the light on the pre-installed hooks. It's compatible with E26 base bulbs, so you can choose your preferred bulb to install (bulb not included). This makes it a great addition to your home without requiring complex setup.
