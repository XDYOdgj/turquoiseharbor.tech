Aspen Creative 23140-11 Etched Alabaster Transitional Glass Shade

Elevate your lighting setup with this elegant etched alabaster glass shade, designed to enhance the aesthetic of your home. Featuring a classic bowl shape and a polished finish, it brings a touch of sophistication to any fixture.

This replacement glass shade is perfect for use with medium base socket lamps, including torchiere, swag, pendant, and flush mount designs. It has a diameter of 15-1/2" and a height of 5-1/2", ensuring a great fit for your lighting needs.

Crafted from high-quality, long-lasting glass, this shade not only improves the look of your existing lighting fixtures but also ensures durability and safe operation. The E26 socket ring is required for installation and is not included with this product.

Ideal for those looking to refresh their lighting decor without replacing the entire fixture, this shade offers an affordable and stylish upgrade. Whether you're setting the mood in your living room or adding flair to your bedroom, this glass shade delivers timeless beauty and modern appeal.

Aspen Creative is dedicated to providing innovative, attractive, and affordable lighting solutions that cater to a wide range of interior styles. From portable lamps to pendant lights, our products are designed with both function and aesthetics in mind.
