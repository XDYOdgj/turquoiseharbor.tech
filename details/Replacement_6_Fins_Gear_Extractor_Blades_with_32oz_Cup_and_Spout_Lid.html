Replacement 6 Fins Gear Extractor Blades with 32oz Cup and Spout Lid - Compatible with Nutri Ninja Models

Upgrade your Nutri Ninja blender with these high-quality replacement 6 Fins Gear Extractor Blades. Designed for easy use and efficient performance, these blades come with a 32oz cup and a spout lid, making them perfect for daily blending tasks. Compatible with a wide range of Nutri Ninja models, including the Nutri Ninja Pro and Auto-iQ series.

The set includes 6 Fins, each with a 4" or 10cm diameter and a weight of 165g. Made from durable and safe food-grade PP and stainless steel materials, these blades are built to last. Ideal for smoothies, shakes, and more, these blades offer a great alternative to original parts.

Note: The rubber gasket in the bottom blade may become loose during shipping. Before using, inspect and ensure it's securely in place for optimal performance.

These blades are not original parts from Nutri Ninja, but they are general replacement parts that work just as well. They provide the same functionality and convenience for all your kitchen needs.
