Rayqueen 52-Inch Ceiling Fan with Lights and Remote

Elevate your living space with this stylish and functional ceiling fan, combining modern design with advanced technology for year-round comfort and efficiency.

Timeless Style for Every Room

The Rayqueen 52-inch ceiling fan brings a sleek, modern look that effortlessly blends with any home decor. Its minimalist design and brushed nickel finish add a touch of elegance to bedrooms, living rooms, or even covered outdoor areas like patios. With dual-sided blades in both sliver and wood grain options, you can choose the style that best matches your interior aesthetic.

Bright and Energy-Efficient Lighting

This ceiling fan is not just for airflow—it also delivers soft, customizable lighting. Featuring a 24W LED light with 2000LM output, it can adjust to three different color temperatures (3000K, 4500K, 6000K) and dimmable brightness (20%-100%). Perfect for creating the right ambiance without using excessive energy.

Silent and Powerful Performance

Equipped with a 6-speed reversible DC motor, the Rayqueen ceiling fan provides powerful ventilation up to 4119 CFM while operating at an ultra-quiet level (below 32dB). Whether you need a refreshing breeze in the summer or a warm, circulating draft in the winter, this fan adapts to your needs for optimal comfort.

Smart & Convenient Remote Control

Enjoy effortless control with the included remote. Adjust speed, brightness, and color temperature with just a few clicks. The fan also features a 1/3/6-hour timer, sleep mode, and reversible airflow to make your life easier, no matter the time of day or season.

Easy to Maintain and Replace

Designed for long-term use, all major components—fan blades, light cover, LED, and remote—are replaceable for added convenience. The modular design simplifies maintenance, and the included installation video ensures a smooth setup process. ETL-certified and supported by 24/7 customer service, this fan is built to last.

Lighting & Fan Kit Includes: Brushed nickel fan, 6-speed reversible DC motor, 3CCT dimmable LED, remote control, and downrod for optimal installation.

Enhance your home's comfort and aesthetics with a Rayqueen ceiling fan that combines style, power, and smart features in one sleek package.
