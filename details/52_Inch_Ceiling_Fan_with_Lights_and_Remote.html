Forrovenco 52" Ceiling Fan with Lights and Remote Control

Elegant, Energy-Efficient, and Perfect for Any Room

Natural Elegance Meets Modern Design

Featuring a stunning combination of matte black metal brackets and handcrafted walnut wood blades, this 52-inch ceiling fan is a stylish addition to any space. The unique, sustainable design is not only visually appealing but also eco-friendly, making it ideal for farmhouse, patio, bedroom, or living room decor.

Simple Installation, Effortless Operation

With a straightforward setup, this fan does not require drilling into your ceiling, preserving the original aesthetics of your room. The included remote control offers convenience and control, allowing you to adjust the 6 speeds, set sleep timers, and change color temperature settings from anywhere in the room.

Advanced DC Motor for Quiet Performance

Equipped with a high-performance DC motor, this fan operates at an ultra-quiet level under 25dB even at full speed. The reverse airflow feature makes it versatile for both summer and winter use, and it also significantly reduces energy consumption compared to traditional AC motors by up to 75%.

Superior Airflow and Reliable Functionality

Providing powerful ventilation, this fan delivers a maximum CFM of 6500 for excellent air circulation. With 3 adjustable downrods (5", 10", and 15"), it can be installed on various ceiling heights. The tilting feature adds flexibility for optimal performance in any setting.

Trusted and Certified Quality

Forrovenco is committed to delivering top-quality products that meet international safety and performance standards. This ceiling fan is ETL and FCC certified, ensuring reliability and safety. Backed by a 3-year technical support guarantee, you can enjoy peace of mind knowing you're getting a durable and efficient product.
