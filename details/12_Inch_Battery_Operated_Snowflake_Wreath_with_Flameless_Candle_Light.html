CW1510A Battery Powered 12 Inch Metal Snowflake Wreath with Hot Spot Flickering Flameless Candle Light

Elevate your home decor with the CW1510A 12-inch metal snowflake window wreath. This elegant and festive decoration is designed to bring warmth and charm to any space, whether it’s for the holidays or year-round.

Featuring an aged bronze finish, this wreath adds a touch of sophistication to your windows, walls, or ceiling. The built-in hot spot flickering flameless candle light illuminates your surroundings with a realistic, warm glow, creating a cozy and inviting atmosphere.

The candle’s LED light source never needs bulb replacement, making it a long-lasting and low-maintenance decoration. Simply twist the base to turn the light on and enjoy 8 hours of warm white illumination each day, followed by 16 hours of rest.

Equipped with an automatic timer, the CW1510A ensures consistent lighting without manual adjustment. It also includes a convenient cordless design, allowing for easy installation in your home without the hassle of wires.

This wreath is the perfect addition to your holiday decor, effortlessly blending traditional Christmas elements with modern, energy-efficient lighting. Whether you're looking to enhance your window display or create a festive ambiance in your living room, this product delivers both style and function.

Dimensions: 11.5" High x 9.5" Wide x 1" Deep. Requires 2 AA Alkaline batteries (not included). Battery life up to 40 days.
