Dynaming 2 Pack Solar Halloween Flame Hanging Lights for Outdoor

Elevate your Halloween decor with these stunning, solar-powered garden lanterns that bring a mysterious flickering flame effect without the need for electricity. Perfect for creating a spooky ambiance in your yard, pathway, porch, or driveway.

Spooky Flame Effect, All Natural

These Halloween lights feature 12 purple SMD LEDs per unit, producing a lifelike flame effect that dances with motion. Whether you're decorating your fence, deck, or garden, these lights add a dramatic and eerie atmosphere. The flame lighting mode is specifically designed to mimic real candles without the fire.

Premium Solar Power, Eco-Friendly

Equipped with solar panels, these lanterns are perfect for outdoor use. Charge them in full sunlight for 6-8 hours and enjoy automatic on/off functionality at night and dawn. Each light includes a 600mAHL NI-MH rechargeable battery, ensuring long-lasting performance with minimal hassle.

Weather-Resistant Design

With an IP44 waterproof rating, these lights are built to withstand light rain, snow, frost, and sleet. Ideal for use in any weather condition, they provide reliable illumination without worrying about damage. The shepherd hooks are 38.7 inches long, allowing for easy placement and secure anchoring.

Simple Setup, Instant Decoration

These lights are designed for easy installation. Follow the included detailed instructions to assemble them in a matter of minutes. Simply screw the poles together, stake them into the ground, and hang the lanterns for a quick and effective Halloween display. For stormy conditions, bring them indoors to protect the solar components.

Enhance Your Outdoor Halloween Experience

Whether you're hosting a festive gathering or adding a touch of Halloween magic to your garden, these lanterns are an essential addition. Their 360-degree viewing angle ensures even lighting across your outdoor space, making every corner feel illuminated and magical.
